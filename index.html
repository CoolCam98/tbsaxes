Who We Are

A little about the Collective.

We're a Tampa Bay saxophone group with a rotating set list and a good time guaranteed. We play everything — jazz, funk, soul, and yes, the occasional Taylor Swift chorus if the wedding calls for it. We're not precious about it. If it sounds good on saxophones, we'll figure out how to play it.

The group started as a handful of saxophone players who wanted something of their own and a drummer who could hold it all together.

We're based in Tampa, we cover most of central Florida without blinking, and we'll travel further if you ask nicely.
