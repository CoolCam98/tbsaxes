We're Now Booking for summer & fall 2026

Our summer and fall calendar is officially open. If you're planning a wedding, a corporate event, a festival, or a private party that deserves more than a Bluetooth speaker, we'd love to hear about it.

What We Play

We're five saxophones and a drummer. No backing tracks, no laptop on stage, no pre-recorded anything — just six musicians playing live in a room together. Our set list covers jazz, funk, soul, pop, R&B, and just about anything else that sounds good on horns. We tailor the repertoire to your event, so tell us the vibe and we'll build a set around it.

What Kind of Events We're Booking

We're comfortable in all kinds of rooms and we know how to read one. Whether the moment calls for background music over dinner, a high-energy dance set, or something in between, we adjust to fit. Here's what we typically play:

  • Wedding ceremonies and receptions
  • Cocktail hours
  • Corporate events and brand activations
  • Music festivals and outdoor stages
  • Private parties and house concerts
  • Restaurant and brewery residencies

Where We Play

We're based in Tampa and cover all of central Florida. Tampa, St. Pete, Clearwater, Sarasota, Lakeland, Orlando, Wesley Chapel, Brandon — if it's within a couple hours of the Bay, we're there. We'll travel further for the right gig too. Just ask.

How to Book

The easiest way is to fill out the booking form on our site. Tell us your date, venue, event type, and any details that help us understand what you're going for. We'll get back to you within a day or two with availability and pricing. You can also reach us directly at tbsaxcollective@gmail.com.

Summer weekends fill up fast — especially June through October. If you already know your date, reach out sooner rather than later so we can hold it for you.

We're not just looking for gigs. We're looking for rooms that want live music done right.

If that sounds like your event, let's talk.
